Top Vacation Spots

1. The Dominican Republic

Nestled in the island of Hispaniola is the Dominican Republic. The island that Columbus landed on. Come and visit for the perfect beach and sunny weather experience!

2. Playa del Carmen

Another paradise for beach lovers! Come stay at a nice resort and enjoy the crystal clear blue waters!

3. Miami Beach

Looking for fun? Hit up spring break's hottest destination to party with your friends! Miami's white beaches and blue waters are for everyone!
